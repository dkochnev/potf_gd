  • About

    Hello! My name is Dmitry Kochnev, and I am a student of City College of New York pursuing a BFA degree in Electronic Design and Multimedia program. I am currently based in New York City. My passion is web design and graphic design. I started to learn web desing 12 years ago. As for graphic design, I work with both 2D and 3D graphics. Having interest in web design and CG for many years, I have had the opportunity to work with most 2D and 3D software and learn a lot of coding tricks. I am mostly comfortable with HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Adobe Creative Suite, 3ds max and Cinema 4D.

    My interests include linguistics, photography, numismatics, painting and kiting. I am inspired by Pixar animation films.
